Song Bio

“What It Iz” by OhGeesy and Lefty Gunplay is a song about money, power, and street life. Both artists deliver hard-hitting verses, highlighting their success and relentless hustle in the street and music life. The song gives listeners the idea of ambition, loyalty, and the drive to never settle for less.
